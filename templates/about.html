What is Whitemoon?

Whitemoon is an application which tells how much a text is seo-friendly based on the focus keyword.

How does it work?

Whitemoon uses algorithms based on the number of words and how many times the focus keyword appears. To get a 100 out of 100, the text must have at least 1000 words and the keyword must account for 2.5% of the whole text.

What are the limitations of Whitemoon?

A maximum SEO score doesn't guarantee that the article will be in the first page of a Google research. That's because There are many other factors which decide how an article or a page will be referenced on search engines. Those include the website's activity and traffic.
